Fresh Home Made Lemonade

Description

This summer, with temperatures rising beyond belief, there's nothing better than a sweet, cold, freshly made lemonade!

And this is what we'll be doing today!

"But isn't lemonade just water, lemon and sugar?" I hear you ask.

It can be, but today we are going to make something different!

Ingredients

  • 2 limes
  • 3 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of sweetened condensed milk
  • ice cubes

Steps

  1. Wash limes thoroughly, cut off the ends and slice into eight wedges.
  2. Place limes in a blender with water, sugar, sweetened condensed milk, and ice.
  3. Pulse 5 times, or until smooth.
  4. Strain through a fine mesh strainer to remove rinds.
  5. Serve over ice.
